Why is it necessary to detect plant diseases?

Early detection of plant diseases is crucial to prevent crop loss. AI-based analysis helps identify diseases before visible symptoms appear, allowing farmers to take timely action.

Prevent Plant Diseases with These Tips:

  • Follow good sanitation practices.
  • Use proper fertilization to keep plants healthy.
  • Inspect plants before bringing them home.
  • Ensure soil is warm before planting.
  • Provide good air circulation.
  • Remove diseased stems and leaves promptly.
